Rate Card & Settings

Outbound GM% formula values · Configurable

Outbound Rate Card
Used for GM% Labor calculation
0.55 = 55%

Formula: GM% = (Revenue − Labor) / Revenue where Revenue = Σ(units × rate). Rate card is shared across all users.

Inbound Rate Card
Used for Inbound GM% Labor calculation

Inbound revenue = Σ(volume × rate) per service type. Labor Rate ($/hr) and GM% Target are shared with Outbound above.

About the Composite Score

Composite = Productivity × 50% + Speed/Velocity × 25% + Utilization × 25%

  • Productivity: CPLH today ÷ 90-day rolling average × 100. If <5 days history, uses all-time average.
  • Speed (Inbound): 90-day avg D2S ÷ today's D2S × 100. Lower D2S is better.
  • Velocity (Outbound): Today's Orders/LaborHr ÷ 90-day average × 100.
  • Utilization: Labor Hours ÷ (Effective × 8 + LoanIn − LoanOut). Non-linear curve penalizes <70% AND >110%.

Status Labels:

  • Excellent Composite ≥110% — exceptional performance
  • Excellent — Lean Crew Composite ≥95% AND running with ≤80% of planned crew
  • Optimal Composite 90–110%
  • Acceptable Composite 75–89%
  • Reduce Crew Low utilization (<70%) but high composite with light volume
  • Light Volume Day Volume <80% of avg, low score
  • Review Staffing Composite <75% — investigate
